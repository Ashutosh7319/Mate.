Install on iOS

iOS Safari does not support one-click downloads. Follow these quick steps to add Mate. to your home screen:

1 Tap the Share button in the bottom toolbar.
2 Scroll down and tap Add to Home Screen .
3 Tap Add in the top-right corner to complete.

Install Mate.

To install Mate. on your laptop or desktop device:

Click the Install Icon in your browser's address bar (URL bar).

Or open your browser settings and select "Install Mate." or "Save as App".
